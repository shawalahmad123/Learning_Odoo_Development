Website Show Price After Login

Mark website products as Exclusive for members only

The module facilitates you to hide the prices of selected website products. Only logged in users or members will be able to see the prices of such products.

Moreover, do you have products or services whose prices depend upon customers' requirements?

Then, the module allows you to unpublish the price of the products and add a contact mail address to the product page. The customer needs to contact the admin to get the price of these products/services.

Features:

  • The module brings the facility to hide the price of all or selected products and services.
  • The setting- ‘Login to View Price’ hides the price so that they are only displayed to the customers who are logged into their website account on Odoo website.
  • The products can be added to the cart only after logging into the website account.
  • The setting- ‘Contact For Price’ hides the price and the customer needs to contact the admin to get the price of the particular product and service.
  • The settings can be applied either at the global level or product level in Odoo.

Use Case Scenarios

Prices for Exclusive Members:

More often than not companies reserve their special products for members only. You can choose to make the products available for only registered members on your Odoo website. Only those who are logged in would be able to see the price and buy the selected products.

Leads generation:

Including contact form or contact mail is one of the most effective. The contact mail allows the admin to collect information from customers. It is highly useful for businesses engaging in renting or leasing products/services.

Price Sensitive Services:

The products and services that whose prices change on a daily or weekly basis depending upon the market fluctuation can be published on the website. The customer would click ‘Contact for Price’ to get the current price of the services or products.
